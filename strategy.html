Our business is Strategy x Innovation

for Senior Management Teams. 

Here is our “take” on these words:

 

Strategy

We facilitate efforts to formulate, update and integrate 

Strategy at Corporate, SBU and Functional levels.

 

Innovation

We help ( the right) ideas happen.  We help through creativity* at the

conceptual level and/or in go-to-market aspects. (*We believe there is

an optimal application of creativity – and that maximum creativity / or

a wide-spread, non-targeted use of creativity can be counter-productive

to “helping ideas happen”.)

 

Senior Management

In efforts that often involve heads (of companies, SBU's and

Functions) and their direct reports, where generally

(and regrettably), creative thought is most often sublimated

to analytical decision-making - we bring both creativity

and closure.

 

Teams

We help the top people collaborate - to expect and

extract value from each other - and to build value together.

 

x

This multiplier means leverage and synergy -  innovation

is most impactful when it occurs at the level of strategy,

when it starts/is modeled at the top, and when it involves

(the right) multiple minds.
